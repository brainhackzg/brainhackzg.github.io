Brainhack Zagreb 2021

Brainhack Zagreb is part of an international initiative of hackathons known as Brainhack Global. We bring researchers from numerous disciplines together to participate in open collaboration at the crossing of neuroscience, computer science and statistics.


How Brainhack works?

Participants will introduce projects and themselves at the beginning of the hackathon. Bring your data, show your work, or ask a question from your field of interest. If you don't have something to show, don't worry, kick back and find what interests you - maybe you can help others in their projects, or learn something new. During open hacking you will have time to work on projects, share ideas, tinker with stuff and most importantly - be the part of the atmosphere!

Travel support available!

We are able to support travel costs for participants coming from outside Zagreb. To apply and for more details, write us at brainhackzg@gmail.com.
