Registration for the Zagreb Hackathon opens soon!

Brainhack Zagreb 2018 is part of the Global Brainhack 2018, with simultaneous hackathons in over 40 sites across the globe. The goal of the hackathon is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds to collaborate on open science projects in neuroimaging.

Dates: 2 days in March 2018

Costs: 20€ EUR, includes onsite breakfast and lunch.

Location: Zagreb, Croatia.

Participants are encouraged to post project ideas to www.brainhack.org and to submit a report describing their project to the Brainhack Proceedings that will be published in 2018 by Research Ideas and Outcomes.

Please join the Brainhack Slack project for updated information on the developing Hackathon content and to contribute your own ideas.
